About Me

Hello, I'm Shivika Tiwari, a professional makeup artist with a passion for creating stunning, transformative looks that enhance natural beauty.

With years of experience in the beauty industry, I specialize in editorial, fashion, beauty, glam, and bridal makeup. My work has been featured in various publications and I've had the privilege of working with talented photographers, models, and brides.

I believe that makeup is an art form that should celebrate individuality. Whether you're preparing for your wedding day, a fashion shoot, or a special event, I work closely with each client to create a personalized look that reflects their unique style and personality.

My kit features premium products from top brands, ensuring flawless results that photograph beautifully and last all day.
