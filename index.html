London Makeup Artist

Hello, I'm Shivika Tiwari, a professional makeup artist based in London with a passion for creating stunning, transformative looks that enhance natural beauty.

With years of experience in the beauty industry working across London and the UK, I specialise in editorial, fashion, beauty, glam, and bridal makeup. My work has been featured in various publications including Vogue, and I've had the privilege of working with talented photographers, models, and brides throughout the city.

Whether you're a bride-to-be in London looking for the perfect wedding day look, a model preparing for a fashion shoot, or someone wanting to feel glamorous for a special event, I work closely with each client to create a personalised look that reflects their unique style and personality.

I travel throughout Greater London, Surrey, Essex, Kent, and surrounding areas. My professional kit features premium products from top brands, ensuring flawless results that photograph beautifully and last all day.
